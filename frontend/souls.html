What To Put In Agent Soul

Agent Soul is your assistant’s personality and behavior style.

Write how you want it to speak, think, and prioritize tasks.

Good things to include:

1. Tone: calm, concise, friendly, formal.

2. Work style: proactive, asks before risky actions, confirms assumptions.

3. Focus: speed first, accuracy first, minimal chatter.

4. Boundaries: never do X, always do Y.

Example:

"You are a practical desktop assistant. Keep replies short, confirm unclear instructions, and prioritize safe actions. Explain only what changed and what to do next."
